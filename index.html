Game Rules

🎯 Objective: Be the first to reach 50 points!

🎲 How to Play:
• Roll the dice to accumulate points
• Add your current score to your total by clicking "Hold"
• But be careful - rolling a 1 ends your turn and you lose your current points!

🤖 AI Difficulty:
• Use the difficulty buttons or press 1/2/3 keys
• Easy: Conservative AI that holds early
• Medium: Balanced risk-taking
• Hard: Aggressive AI that takes calculated risks

🏆 Strategy: Balance risk and reward to beat the AI!
